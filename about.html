About the Portal

The University of the West Indies Gym Portal was developed to improve access, scheduling, and management of gym equipment for students, staff, and faculty.

Efficient Booking

Reserve equipment in advance and reduce waiting times during peak hours.

Better Management

Track equipment demand, maintenance needs, and booking activity.

Inclusive Access

Supports students, staff, and faculty members across the university.
